The Exercises sit at the edge of this library, and deliberately so. Ignatius is not a nondual teacher: the Exercises are a structured month of imaginative, devotional prayer, and their frame is entirely dualist, the creature before the Creator. They are included because of where they reliably lead. The discipline of attention they train, the indifference they cultivate toward everything except what is essential, and the *Contemplation to Attain Love* that closes the month, in which God is found labouring in all created things, have carried generations of retreatants to the threshold the apophatic mystics describe from the other side. Read them as the most rigorous Western manual for the preparation of attention, and the company they keep here makes sense.
